Everything you need to know

Neutrogena® Ultra Sheer Dry-Touch Sunblock
SPF 50+
has Helioplex Technology which offers powerful protection against sun damage while leaving a weightlessly invisible feeling on your skin.

  • Feels Clean & Light: Goes on easily and absorbs quickly for an invisible, weightless feel on your skin.
  • Leaves A Non-Shiny Finish: The oil-free formula leaves a non-greasy, matte finish so your skin is shine-free.
  • Won't Clog Pores: PABA free and non-comedogenic.

Why do you need Helioplex broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection?

There are two types of UV rays that can damage the health and beauty of your skin, they are UVA and UVB rays. For the most comprehensive sun protection against UVA and UVB rays, you need a broad-spectrum sunscreen that can help protect your skin against burning, signs of ageing, and skin cancer.
